# credit-card-customer-acquisition-and-segmentation
Customer segmentation and credit card targeting analysis using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

## Summary

This project analyzes customer demographics, income, and spending behavior to identify high-value credit card prospects.

An end-to-end workflow was implemented:

Python (data cleaning) → SQL (analysis and segmentation) → Power BI (dashboard visualization)

The goal is to derive actionable insights for customer acquisition, segmentation, and revenue optimization.



## Business Objective

- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Analyze spending and income utilization patterns  
- Support credit card targeting strategies  
- Maximize revenue while managing risk  



## Tools and Technologies

- Python (Pandas) – Data cleaning  
- SQL – Aggregations, CTEs, analysis  
- Power BI – Dashboard visualization  



## Key Insights

- High utilization customers (~30%) contribute approximately 50% of total revenue  
- Medium segment (~55%) is the largest and best growth opportunity  
- Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi NCR) drive the highest spending  
- Salaried IT professionals are the most valuable segment  
- Customers already show strong credit usage behavior  



## Segmentation Strategy

- Premium Segment: High income, high utilization → Premium credit cards  
- Growth Segment: Medium utilization → Cashback and EMI-based cards  
- Expansion Segment: Underutilized groups → Business-focused cards  



## Outcome

This project demonstrates how data can be used to:

- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Design targeted credit card strategies  
- Support data-driven business decisions
